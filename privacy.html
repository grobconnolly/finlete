Finlete Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Finlete. We respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, www.finlete.com, use our mobile application, or engage with our services.

2. Information Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you register on the Platform, engage in transactions, or communicate with us. This information may include:

  • Personal identification information: Name, email address, postal address, phone number.
  • Communications: Your communications with Finlete’s support team.
  • Transaction information: Details about purchases of Finlete shares and payment information.

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our Platform, such as:

  • Log and usage data: Information about how you interact with our services.
  • Device information: Information about the device and internet connection you use to access our services including: IP address, browser type, and operating system.

3. Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services.
  • To process transactions.
  • For customer support and to respond to your inquiries.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on our Platform.
  • For marketing purposes, subject to your consent when required by law.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing and data analysis.
  • – Law enforcement, government officials, United States or international courts, or other third parties when compelled to do so by subpoena, court order, or equivalent legal procedures, when necessary to comply with legal obligations requited by law.

5. Data Security

We, or the third party systems on which we rely, have implemented security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information; however, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding the collection and storage of your personal data, including:

  • The right to access, update or delete your personal information that we have collected or stored when you access our platform.
  • The right to object to processing of your data, request data portability, and withdraw consent for data processing.

7. Third-Party Websites

Our Platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@finlete.com.

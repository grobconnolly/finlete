AVG

Batting Average represents how often a player records a hit. It's calculated by dividing the total number of hits divided by total number of at-bats, also represented as H/AB.

SLG

Slugging Percentage represents the total number of bases a player records per at-bat. It's calculated by taking the sum of the number of singles + number of doubles multiplied by 2 + number of triple multiples by 3 + number of home runs multiplied by 4 divided by total number of at-bats, also represented as (1B + 2Bx2 + 3Bx3 + HRx4)/AB).

OPS

OPS stands for On-base Plus Slugging. It adds on-base percentage and slugging percentage. It's meant to combine how well a hitter can reach base with how well he can hit for average and for power.

wRC+

wRC+ takes Runs Created* and adjusts it to account for important external factors like ballpark or era. wRC+ of 100 is league average and 150 would be 50% above league average. *TB x (H + BB) / (AB + BB)

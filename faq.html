What is Finlete?

Finlete is a groundbreaking platform enabling promising athletes to sell a percentage of their future earnings to fans. Athletes receive capital and superfans to support their journey, fans gain access to unique financial upside as well as exclusive perks. Together, we’re creating a community of athletes and fans unlike any other.

Can I really make money?

Yes! After you buy a stake in an athlete on Finlete, you make money every time that athlete gets paid a professional sports wage, such as a Major League Baseball (MLB) paycheck. Just like when you buy a stock on the stock market, however, it’s important to know financial returns are not guaranteed. It all depends on how much the athlete makes over the course of their professional sports career.

What do you mean by a community?

When a fan supports an athlete in exchange for future financial upside, the relationship doesn’t end there – it’s just getting started. By joining Finlete, athletes gain a following that’s so much stronger than, say, their Instagram followers. Fans on Finlete are making a much deeper commitment to an athlete than just liking their posts, they’re truly invested in the athlete’s journey to stardom both emotionally and financially. In turn, Finlete helps the athlete offer exclusive experiences, connections, interactions, content, merchandise, and more through our website.

How does Finlete benefit athletes financially?

Finlete offers athletes a way to de-risk their journey to the professional ranks by getting paid now in exchange for a percentage of their future earnings. Finlete ensures athletes receive fair valuations based on their projected professional sports career earnings. This alternative to traditional funding sources allows athletes greater control over their financial futures.

Can any sports fan participate in Finlete?

Yes! Finlete is accessible to both accredited and non-accredited investors, making this unique opportunity accessible to as many people as possible. We operate under SEC regulations for crowdfunding and adhere to FINRA oversight, ensuring a secure experience for all.

Why was Finlete created?

Finlete was created to disrupt the traditional athlete funding model, offering a system that’s more fair to the athlete and accessible to sports fans, not just hedge funds. We’re passionate not only about bringing athletes and fans together for the financial opportunity but also for the real sense of community it’ll foster.

How does Finlete pick the athletes?

Finlete utilizes various sources of expert analysis including scouts, rankings, data, and more to determine which athletes to sign. We focus on doing business with athletes with bright futures ahead of them and who are also excited to build a following and interact with fans through Finlete.

How can I join Finlete as an athlete?

To join Finlete as an athlete, start by emailing us at athletes@finlete.com. We'll guide you through our evaluation process.

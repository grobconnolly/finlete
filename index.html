Invest in the world's most promising athletes

Finlete has future income agreements with promising athletes. We allow you to invest in the future of these athletes. You share in the financial success of your prospects.

Choose the prospect

Select a prospect we have available on our platform. Review terms, watch practice films, and review scouting reports.

Purchase Shares

If you are interested in investing in that athlete, select the amount you want to invest, and complete your transaction.

Get Perks

The more you invest, the more perks you will receive. Items include private athlete pages, signed merch, private virtual Q&A sessions, meet-and-greets, and other awesome items.

Get Paid

If your athletes make it to the pros, you will start receiving a % of that player's paychecks through a future earnings contract. The amount you receive will be based on how many shares you have and the dollar amount of their contract.

Invest in the world's most promising athletes

Finlete has future earning agreements with promising athletes. We register our contracts with the SEC through a Reg A+. Fans that invest in athletes with Finlete own a percentage of their future on-the-field earnings. Investing in one of our athletes allows you to share in the journey emotionally and financially.

Choose the prospect

Select a prospect we have available on our platform. Review terms, watch practice films, and review scouting reports.

Purchase Shares

If you are interested in investing in that athlete, select the amount you want to invest and complete your transaction.

Get Perks

The more you invest, the more perks you will receive. Items include private athlete pages and other extraordinary items.

Get Paid

If your athletes make it to the pros, you'll start receiving a % of that player's paychecks through a future earnings contract.

Jakob Marsee, a rising star in the Padres organization, stands out for his exceptional hit tool and on-base prowess. A product of Central Michigan, he's a 2023 AFL MVP with a remarkable .420/.533/.754 slash line. Known for high-contact rates and disciplined plate approach, Marsee combines speed, defensive versatility, and innate batting intelligence. Although he's developing in power, his consistent performance and work ethic signal a bright future in baseball.

Jakob Marsee is a dynamic outfielder with a strong hit tool, showcasing high contact rates and plate discipline. His speed is an asset both on the bases and in the field. While he's developing power, his batting intelligence and consistent performance mark him as a promising talent.
